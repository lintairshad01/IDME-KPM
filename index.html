IDME KPM Sistem | Log Masuk & Pendaftaran 2025

Access the Ministry of Education's identity management system for teachers and staff. This portal centralizes login, registration, and verification services for a seamless experience in 2025. Learn the complete process of creating an account, logging in, and verifying your credentials.

Overview

The IDME KPM Sistem serves as a centralized platform for educators and administrative staff, enabling single sign-on to access Ministry of Education services, official documents, and communications. With updated 2025 protocols, the portal emphasizes strong verification, modern authentication, and a user-friendly registration experience.

Registration (Pendaftaran)

Visit idme.moe.gov.my to start registration. Fill in personal details, employment information, and valid contact numbers for OTP verification. You may also need to upload identity documents, such as staff ID or appointment letters. After submission, your account undergoes verification by designated administrators before activation.

Login (Log Masuk)

Once registered, log in using your email or staff ID and password. The system supports multi-factor authentication using OTPs via SMS or authenticator apps. If your organization has single sign-on enabled, you can use official credentials linked to your institution.

Verification & Security

Ensure all uploaded documents are valid and readable. Enable two-factor authentication and maintain a unique password. Update your email or phone number promptly to avoid login issues. Verification is essential for securing access and safeguarding your account.

Quick Steps

Step 1: Go to idme.moe.gov.my and select "Pendaftaran".
Step 2: Fill in your personal and employment details, then submit necessary documents.
Step 3: Verify your account via email or OTP to complete registration.

Support & Best Practices

Use your official institution email when registering. Save recovery codes, enable multi-factor authentication, and regularly check your linked devices. For issues, contact your school administrator or portal support to ensure smooth access to IDME KPM Sistem.
